Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: PlanAWalk is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed with privacy-first principles, storing all your data locally on your device.

1. Information We Collect

Health Data

PlanAWalk accesses the following health data from Apple HealthKit with your explicit permission:

  • Walking & Running Distance - To track your walking progress
  • Step Count - To measure steps taken during walks
  • Workouts - To identify and record walking activities

Goal & Progress Data

The app stores locally on your device:

  • Your walking goals (target, duration, frequency)
  • Progress toward your goals
  • Badges earned
  • Walking streak information
  • Walking history dates

2. How We Use Your Information

Your health and activity data is used exclusively to:

  • Track your progress toward walking goals
  • Calculate statistics (steps, miles, time)
  • Award achievement badges
  • Maintain your walking streak
  • Provide motivational messages and reminders

Important: All processing happens entirely on your device. We never transmit your health data to external servers or third parties.

3. Data Storage

Local Storage Only

PlanAWalk stores all data locally on your device using:

  • HealthKit - Your health data remains in Apple's Health app
  • Local Files - Goals, badges, and statistics are saved in your device's app storage

No Cloud Storage

We do not:

  • Upload your data to cloud servers
  • Create backups on external servers
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Store your data anywhere except your device

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties.

Your health information:

  • Remains on your device
  • Is not sent to analytics services
  • Is not used for advertising
  • Is not shared with other apps (except as you explicitly allow through iOS)

5. Data Security

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS Security - Apple's built-in encryption and security features
  • HealthKit Privacy - Apple's strict HealthKit privacy and security framework
  • Device Security - Your device's passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID
  • No Network Transmission - Data never leaves your device

6. HealthKit Permissions

PlanAWalk requests access to specific HealthKit data types. You can:

  • Grant or deny access at any time
  • Change permissions in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → PlanAWalk
  • Revoke access without losing your goal and badge data

Without HealthKit access, the app cannot track your walking activities, but your previously earned badges and statistics will remain.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Current Goal - Until you delete it or create a new one
  • Badges - Permanently stored until you delete the app
  • Statistics - Maintained as long as the app is installed
  • HealthKit Data - Managed by Apple's Health app, not by PlanAWalk

Deleting Your Data

To delete your data:

  • Delete Current Goal - Use the delete option in Settings or Home screen menu
  • Delete All App Data - Uninstall PlanAWalk from your device
  • Remove HealthKit Access - Revoke permissions in iOS Settings

8. Children's Privacy

PlanAWalk does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general audiences and relies on HealthKit data, which requires parental consent for users under 13 as per Apple's policies.

9. Third-Party Services

PlanAWalk does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app operates entirely offline and does not make network requests.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Providing an in-app notice for significant changes

Your continued use of PlanAWalk after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access - View all data stored by the app
  • Modify - Edit your goals and settings at any time
  • Delete - Remove your data by deleting goals or uninstalling the app
  • Control - Manage HealthKit permissions in iOS Settings
  • Transparency - Understand exactly what data we access and how we use it

12. Legal Compliance

PlanAWalk complies with:

  • Apple's HealthKit Review Guidelines
  • Apple's App Store Review Guidelines
  • Applicable privacy laws and regulations
